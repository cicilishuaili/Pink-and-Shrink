 Product Features:  Movement: Mechanical Hand Wind  Diameter: About 4.8 cm  Thickness: About 1.6 cm  Total length of chain: About 30 cm   Package Content:  1 x Pocket Watch  1 x Chain    How to Use Mechanical Pocket Watch?   1.  Setting time： Pull the crown away from base of the watch and turn the crown clockwise until reaching the exact time.  2. Winding the watch： Push the crown back into place along the base of the watch and winding up the watch clockwise rotation with 20 times. The power will be fulled.  Do this one time each day (before sleeping). Otherwise, it will stop working.   Note:  Please do not over winding up the watch if you feel the crown is tight.
