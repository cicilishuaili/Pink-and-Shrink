Setting time Press the crown at the top of watch, the front plate will open.  Pull the crown away from base of the watch and turn the crown clockwise until reaching the exact time. B. Winding the watch Push the crown back into place along the base of the watch and winding up the watch  clockwise rotation with 20-30 turns. The power will be full(Over winding will ultimately  break the spring). Do this one time each day (before sleeping). Otherwise, it will stop  working.
