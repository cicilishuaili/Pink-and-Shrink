This lovely pocket watch is the perfect finishing accessory for many costumes. The chain has a fishhook clasp to attach securely to the shank of a button or vest pocket. The chain is designed to drape across the body into a vest or trouser pocket. The watch itself is made of solid brass with a white face. The black roman numerals print make it easy to read.
