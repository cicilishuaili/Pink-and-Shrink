The gold tone Charles-Hubert 3869G is a pocket watch from the classic collection. The gold tone case has an antiqued finish with an open face and coin-edge. Inside is  a 17-jewel (shock-protected) mechanical hand wind movement. Features a mineral crystal front and back, a chapter ring with black roman numerals, black hands, seconds sub-dial at 9:00, and a 47mm gold tone case with see-into back. Includes a gold tone watch chain and a gift box. Case: Gold tone plated brass, 47mm diameter, 14.5mm thick. Chain: Plated metal watch chain. Warranty: Lifetime factory warranty.
