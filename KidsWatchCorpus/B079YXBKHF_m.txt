This watch is one of a kind for any child and is brand new straight from the maker. The face has on it graphics of Mario, Luigi, Yoshi and Toad. The band has a graphic design throughout the entire thing and is adjustable to the size of your wrist with 8 holes. It can go from 6 to 8 inches. There is also a feature when you press the corner button, you will see a flashing circular light in an array of colors across the face for 10 seconds.
