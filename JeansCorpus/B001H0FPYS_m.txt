The Levi's 550 Jean is relaxed fit with one of the most comfortable fits you will find in denim. This jean is made from 13.5 oz. 100% cotton and offers an easy universal fit through the seat and thigh. The Levi's 550 Jean sits below the waist with a slightly tapered leg and 16 1/2-inch leg openings. You will find a front zippered with button closure and five pocket styling for all your storage needs.
