The lightest shoe in the Hyper series family, the HYPER MD 5 weighs in at 6.4 ounces with pins making it an ideal shoe for a variety of events including jumps. Using a newly designed super lightweight upper featuring hotmelt TPU construction and monomesh, combined with a Nylon Spike plate and cushioned heel, the Hyper MD 5 is the perfect shoe for those that want to fly- either across the track or thru the air.
