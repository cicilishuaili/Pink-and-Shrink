The Throw Pro Track Shoe from ASICS is designed for heavy-weight throwers. It has a mesh upper for breathable comfort. The shoe has a lace-up closure with a hook and loop strap over it for a secure fit. It features adjustments to the sole geometry and rubber compound for cushioning comfort. This shoe has a midfoot support strap to help keep your foot locked. It has a board-lasted innersole for improved stability. While designed with specifications from spin-technique throwers, it still caters to glide throwers, making this shoe the secret weapon for record breaking throws.
