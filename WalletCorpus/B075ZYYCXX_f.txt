Check your wallet You might not know it, but you could have a credit or debit card that uses a tiny computer chip and a radio antenna to transmit account information from your card even when you're not shopping. The cards are designed to be convenient, but they are also vulnerable to being skimmed without ever leaving your pocket.  This compact but mighty wallet features RFID blocking technology to guard your credit cards from identity thieves with external scanning devices, or electronic pick-pocketing. Your information and identity can easily be taken from you, this wallet will prevent that. Stay safe!
