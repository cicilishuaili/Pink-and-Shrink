The Parker 29L is our "unisex" model safety razor. The long thin handle is excellent for women who prefer a double edge safety razor for shaving or for men who like a long handled butterfly razor. The "sure grip" pattern is excellent for shaving in the shower or with wet hands. This razor has a traditional butterfly open twist-to-open design and it comes with 5 Shark Super Chrome blades to deliver a smooth and comfortable shave every time!
