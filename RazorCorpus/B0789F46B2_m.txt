Instruction for Shaving              Softening hair by water or wet towel, then apply some shaving cream on your skin.    Keep the angle of blade and skin about 25 to 35 degrees.     Keep your skin as tension as possible.    Shave against the direction of hair growth.  Note: Our razor is shave ready when out of box, if in case you feel it is not sharp enough, only need some passes on a strop, it is really make difference.   Maintenance             After shaving each time, keep the blade clean and dry with rag.    Have a good strop to keep the blade sharp.
