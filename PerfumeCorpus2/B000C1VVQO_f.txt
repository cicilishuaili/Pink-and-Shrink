Introduced in 1996. Fragrance notes: citrus, pineapple, and woody notes, blended with the scent of ocean air. Recommended use: casual.When applying any fragrance please consider that there are several factors which can affect the natural smell of your skin and, in turn, the way a scent smells on you.  For instance, your mood, stress level, age, body chemistry, diet, and current medications may all alter the scents you wear.  Similarly, factor such as dry or oily skin can even affect the amount of time a fragrance will last after being applied. In the top note , fresh, juicy notes of watermelon and pineapple introduce the delicate energy of the heart. In the heart note , the delicate energy is revealed by a floral accord led by lily of the valley. In the base note, the Eau de Toilette dries down to a seductive finish of blackcurrant and sensual woods
