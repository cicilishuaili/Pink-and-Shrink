A citrus aromatic fragrance for women and men. Crisp, clean, sweet, bright and uplifting. Top notes are orange oil, peach, basil, bergamot and lemon . Middle notes are cyclamen, lily, melon, jasmine and Bulgarian rose. Base notes are patchouli, Tahitian vetiver, musk, sandalwood, oak moss and cedar. Suitable for daytime or warmer seasons wear.
