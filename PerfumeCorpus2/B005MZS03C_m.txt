A woody aromatic fragrance for men. Intense, strong, provocative and seductive. Top notes of amalfi lemon, coriander and lavender . Heart notes of orange blossom and neroli . Base notes of patchouli, cedar and amber. Launched in 2011. Recommended for nighttime wear. Guilty Pour Homme Eau De Toilette spray is designed for dynamic men who are not afraid to stand out from the crowd. Introduced by the design house of Gucci in 2011, this invigorating fragrance combines herbal and zesty top notes of lavender, Amalfi lemon and African orange flower with earthy base notes of patchouli and Virginia cedar to create a stimulating, lasting aroma. Spray on some Guilty Pour Homme and feel fresh and focused all day, from your early morning run to your evening out with friends.
