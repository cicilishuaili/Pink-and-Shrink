Be Fresh. Dive Deep. Make a Splash. Perry Ellis AQUA EXTREME is a modern masculine blend of zesty citrus and fresh aromatic notes contrasted with rich, sensual woods. An initial burst of sheer bergamot, luminous aquatics, and verbena evolve into extreme notes of geranium, blue lavender, and juniper. The addictive trail of the fragrance evokes a sophisticated warmth of rich labdanum, tobacco leaf, and milk wood.
