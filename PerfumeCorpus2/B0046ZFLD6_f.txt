Lily Of The Valley for Women Eau De Toilette Spray 4.2 Oz / 125 Ml by Yardley Of London. Lily of the Valley by Woods of Windsor is a classic scent for women's daytime usage. It was introduced in 1978 and still retains its credibility. This parfum de toilette has been produced by blending floral and green notes together in its top; citrusy and musky notes in its middle and white floral notes comprising its base. This adorable scent is suitable for going to the office to be in constant touch with a garden.
