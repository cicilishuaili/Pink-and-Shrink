A fragrance of an elegant alchemy combining a fresh top and a sensuous base. The fragrance has a fresh and tonic top where Bergamot is mingled with fruity Pineapple. An aromatic heart of Lavender is interlaced with White Jasmine flowers and enhanced by crisp Apple. Sensual background is emphasized with opulence of Cedarwood and Patchouli where warmth of sweet Amber radiates around Musks.
