Taken from the elusive Dolce and Gabbana Anthology fragrance collection, 3 L'Imperatrice Eau de Toilettte is an iconic scent inspired by tarot cards and their marks.Fronted by a Mario Testino advertising campaign, Dolce and Gabbana 3 L Imperatrice is a sophisticated and stylish scent which features notes of Watermelon, Kiwi, Pink Cyclamen and Musk.
