Introduced by Hanae Mori in 1995, the eponymous feminine creation boasts a decadent collection of aromatic notes and a signature Oriental flair. Jasmine adds a heady and seductive floral note to sweet, luscious fruit essences of blackberry, blueberry, wild strawberry and black currant. A splash of this enticing women's fragrance sweeps you away to a world of exotic fantasy and romance.
