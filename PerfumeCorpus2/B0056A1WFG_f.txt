Vera Wang Lovestruck for Women Eau De Parfum Spray 3.4 Oz / 100 Ml by Vera Wang. Lovestruck is another beauty produced by the Vera Wang house of perfumes in 2011. The notes for this beautiful feminine scent have been gathered from Succulent guavas and garden-fresh flowers of angelica; then they are treated with woody notes and musk accords to produce the ideal long lasting and alluring effect in the woman wearing it. This is not overpowering and lasts quite longer.
