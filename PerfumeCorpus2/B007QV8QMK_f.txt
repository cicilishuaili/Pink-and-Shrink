This was launched by the design house of Betsey Johnson in the year 2006.The nose behind this fragrance is Bernard Blanc and Mathilde Bijaoui.Top notes are Grapefruit, pear, black currant and tangerine;middle notes are Floral and fruity accords of freesia, lily of the valley and red apple.Base notes areCedarwood, sandalwood, praline, amber and musks.This fragrance is classified as Fruity.
