Adidas moves for her is an energetic floral fragrance for active women who seize the day and live life to the fullest. Top notes grapefruit, fresh green apple, black peppercorn, ounce one, peppermint, tomato leaf accord, cyclamen, and tagete. Heart notes juniper, cedarwood, lavender, rose, mandarin, waterfall freesia, and geranium. Base notes sheer woods, green notes and musk.
