This hard-shell collection includes four 20ml bottles of the top fragrances for men by English Laundry--Arrogant, Riviera, Notting Hill and Oxford Bleu. Each scent takes on a unique charm and character: The sophistication of Riviera (nutmeg, black pepper, oak moss), the spicy panache of Arrogant (bergamot, coriander, amber), the classic clean of Notting Hill (lemon, peppercorn, cedarwood), or the captivating Oxford Bleu (geranium, sandalwood, vanilla). The small sizes make these bottles perfect for traveling.
