New original boxed Ralph Lauren Polo Sport Eau de Toilette for Men, 4.2 fl oz/ 125 ml. Polo Sport by Ralph Lauren is a Aromatic Green fragrance for men. Polo Sport was launched in 1994. The nose behind this fragrance is Harry Fremont. Top notes are aldehydes, artemisia, lavender, mandarin orange, mint, neroli, bergamot and lemon; middle notes are cyclamen, ginger, seagrass, jasmine, rose, brazilian rosewood and geranium; base notes are sandalwood, amber, musk, guaiac wood and cedar.
