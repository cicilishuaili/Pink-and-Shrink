Launched by the design house of Bvlgari in 2005, BVLGARI OMNIA CRYSTALLINE is a women's fragrance that possesses a blend of balsa wood, bamboo, lotus flower, and nashi. It is recommended for casual wear. When applying any fragrance please consider that there are several factors which can affect the natural smell of your skin and, in turn, the way a scent smells on you.  For instance, your mood, stress level, age, body chemistry, diet, and current medications may all alter the scents you wear.  Similarly, factor such as dry or oily skin can even affect the amount of time a fragrance will last after being applied
