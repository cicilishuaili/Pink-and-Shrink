A+ Attention contains the 100% pure KidSafe Essential Oils of Petitgrain, Bergamot, Cedarwood Atlas, Pink Grapefruit, Lavender, and Vetiver. These oils together create a balancing, calming synergy blend that helps children focus on the task at hand. There are so many distractions that children face, and when it comes to focusing on a specific task they can struggle. Use A+ Attention in a personal inhaler for children to inhale the synergy blend or use diluted in a carrier oil and apply to the chest, back of neck, and/or wrists to help encourage your child(ren) to focus.
