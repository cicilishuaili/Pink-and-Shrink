An aromatic fougere fragrance for modern men. Classy, crisp, spicy, clean, warm and uplifting. Top notes of Sichuan pepper and Calabrian bergamot. Heart notes of geranium, lavender, elemi, pink pepper, vetiver and patchouli . Base notes of cedar, labdanum and ambroxan. Launched in 2015. Recommended for spring or summer wear.
