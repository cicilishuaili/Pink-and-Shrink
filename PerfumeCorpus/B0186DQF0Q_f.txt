Moments of euphoric joy can sometimes surprise us. It can be as simple as seeing an old friend or laughing at a funny commentary. Uplift was crafted to remind us of those simple, yet happy, moments. Possessing a citrusy aroma with hints of peppermint, Uplift is a blend of Bergamot, Lemon, Melissa, Orange, Peppermint, Tangerine and Ylang Ylang. Exotic and supple, Uplift can help lift us out of tired and low emotions to help us embrace joy. As always, Edens Garden essential oils are never diluted or mixed with additives. CONTAINS: Bergamot (Citrus bergamia), Lemon (Citrus medica limonum), Melissa (Melissa officinalis), Orange (Citrus aurantium dulcis), Peppermint (Mentha piperita), Tangerine (Citrus reticulata) and Ylang Ylang (Cananga odorata)
