Our lavender fragrance is a tranquil combination of fresh herbs and cool camphor. It begins with top notes of Italian bergamot, eucalyptus leaves, citrus zest, and French lavender; followed by middle notes of lily of the valley, and clary sage; sitting on base notes of Nordic pine, cool camphor, and clove buds. This fragrance is phthalate free.
