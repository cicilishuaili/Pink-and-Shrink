Breathing can sometimes be difficult with all of the stuff that we come into contact with everyday. From congestion to worries, we are faced with a plethora of problems that can affect our breathing. Plant Therapy's Respire Aid was created using only the purest of undiluted essential oils to create a synergy blend that would ease congestion and allow its user to breathe easier. This soothing, unique blend includes Eucalyptus, Pine, Peppermint, Lavender, Spruce, Cypress, and Marjoram to create a calming, comforting, and invigorating blend that supports the respiratory system. By creating a steam bath or a chest rub Respire Aid synergy blend is sure to help you relax, and breathe easier.
