Ferrari Black for Men Eau De Toilette Spray 4.2 Oz / 125 Ml by Ferrari. Ferrari issued in 1999 their branded fragrance for men by the name of Ferrari Black. This citric and sensual scent is sweet and strong with fruity notes and emanates masculinity. Top notes have been taken from lime, plum, red apple and bergamot; the middle ones from cardamom, rose, cinnamon and jasmine plus spice and florals and the base notes from cedar, musk, amber and vanilla.
