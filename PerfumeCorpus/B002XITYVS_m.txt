This was launched by the design house of Zino Davidoff in the year 1988.The nose behind this fragrance is Pierre Bourdon. Top notes are Mint and green nuances, lavender, coriander, and rosemary; middle notes are Geranium, neroli, jasmine, and sandalwood. Base notes are Cedarwood, musk, amber, and tobacco. This fragrance is classified as Floral.
