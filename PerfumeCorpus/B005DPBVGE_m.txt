This was launched by the design house of Antonio Puig in the year 1982.The nose behind this fragrance is Antonio Puig. Top notes are artemisia, caraway, grapefruit, bergamot and lemon; middle notes are cyclamen, pine tree, carnation, sandalwood, patchouli and jasmine. Base notes are leather, amber, oakmoss and tobacco. This fragrance is classified as Woody Aromatic .
