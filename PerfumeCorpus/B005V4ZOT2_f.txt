Frankincense Serrata, also known as Olibanum, has a wonderfully calming, earthy scent. Plant Therapy's Frankincense Serrata is steam-distilled from the gum resin of the frankincense tree and is 100% pure with no fillers or additives. This oil has been used for thousands of years in cultures around the world in ceremonies and spiritual gatherings. It is also known for its ability to keep skin smooth and young-looking. Whether diffused or diluted with a carrier oil and applied topically, frankincense is a wonderful addition to any essential oil collection. It blends well with Bergamot, Carrot seed, Cedarwood Atlas, Clove, Lemon, Myrrh, Nutmeg, Orange, Pine, Sandalwood, Spruce and Vetiver.
