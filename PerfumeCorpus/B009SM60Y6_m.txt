Product DescriptionVersace Man Eau Fraiche by Gianni Versace is the right daytime cologne for the man looking to make an impression. Introduced in 2006, the fragrance features a number of notes, including a mix of lemon, carambola, rosewood, tarragon, musk and sage, to create a scent that will help get you through your day. The cologne provides you with flexibility to wear it in a number of situations, such as an afternoon out with your family or a day at the office.
