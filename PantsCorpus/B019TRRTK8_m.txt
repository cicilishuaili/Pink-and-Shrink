The Insulated Snow Pants from Natural Gear will keep you dry and concealed on those snowy days in the field. These pants are constructed from waterproof DrilStalk III material and all seams are fully taped for 100% waterproof performance. With 100 grams of insulation and the reverse Tricot lining these pants will keep you warm and comfortable in the harshest conditions. There are two zippered front pockets and one zippered rear pocket to keep your gear secure and dry, and the knee high leg zippers will allow you to easily slip these pants on over your existing clothing. These pants are perfect for predator, big game, or waterfowl hunting in snowy conditions.
