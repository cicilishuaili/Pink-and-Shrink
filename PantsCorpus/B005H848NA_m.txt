From mountain trails to sunny river banks to scorching deserts, the Trail Convertible Pant swiftly converts from pants to ten-inch inseam shorts when conditions change. Made of a UPF30 sun protection woven nylon for quick dry and travel friendly pants that also have a Teflon fabric protector and flat front with elasticated side waist. The pants offer button opening with fly front closure, attached web belt, legs quickly zip-off to convert to shorts. Details: Cargo pockets with touch fastener closure, saddle gusset, expandable leg opening with zipper and touch fastener closure, shorts inseam approximately 10'' and pants inseam is approximately 32''. Material: Nylon.
