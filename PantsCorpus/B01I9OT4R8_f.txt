The Erin Snow Parker pant is the finest combination of fit, performance and Eco responsibility. The outer fabric is made from 100% recycled polyester, the waterproof membrane is bluesign approved and 100% recyclable (similar to a pet bottle) and the insulation was manufactured in the USA from recycled polyester. These pants will fit beautifully over you ski boot, keep you exceptionally dry and warm with an beautiful fit and level of comfort.
