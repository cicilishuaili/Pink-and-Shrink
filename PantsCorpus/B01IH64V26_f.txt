The Sprite 3/4 tights are ultralight eight stretch tights made with our core spun fabric and a versatile 3/4 Capri length leg. The Sprite 3/4 tights feature a light, highly breathable design perfect for layering under your winter outerwear, a dress, or a skirt. These tights feature our core spun fabric which uses nylon fibers wrapped in Merino wool for added durability and strength that maintain next-to-skin softness. The unique stitching design features no side seams, and no crotch seams, making for the ultimate in chafe-free comfort. The wrapped elastic waist makes for clean and comfortable style.
