Yogipace-SUN PROTECTION COLLECTIONWe must face the fact that 90% of skin cancers and premature aging are a result of UV exposure. According to the Skin Cancer Foundation: Clothing to be the most safe and effective defense in the fight against skin cancer and aging.The Ultraviolet Protection Factor (UPF) rating system measures the UV protection provided by fabric. It is similar to SPF (Sun Protection Factor), the rating system used for sunscreen products.A garment with UPF of 50 only allows 1/50th of the UV radiation falling on the surface ofthe garment to pass through it. In other words, it blocks 49/50ths or 98% of the UV radiation.Yogiace SUN PROTECTION COLLECTION garments rate UPF 50+, which is the highest possible rating. Our fabrics are thoroughly tested at independent labs to ensure each product exceeds our UPF 50+ standard.Our garments have good skin coverage and provide excellent sun protection for you. Just enjoy your outdoor activities.
